About Us


A project made by The Nishachars, as we call ourselves, comprising of Aniruddh Muley, Dishant Vyas and Shrey Mavani, is a solution to a problem which almost every institute or event manager faces, but not resolved- The issues of managing the registration of attendees. The managers and commitees have a large team to work on these, but still there are chances of human error. College Fests and programs show the face and the reputation of the institute, and the shows, that of the artists performing. One small error can cause a great havoc for the organisers and the crowd.

Here we are with the solution. We offer a platform for the organisers to register themselves with us and put up their events on our platform. As the registrations start, the organizer will be shown the details of the attendees, who will be given a Uniquely Generated ID (UGID) for the event, and the required analytics to get an overview of the count.
